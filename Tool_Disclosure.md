## Disclosure Statement

AI was used meaningfully, purposefully, and transparently throughout the development of Ùlẹ̀rà AI.

The following were entirely the original work of the developer and were not generated by AI:

- The problem identification — drawn from 5 years of lived experience as a licensed CHEW in Oyo State
- The concept and product direction — the three-mode structure, the burnout detection approach using indirect behavioural questioning, the HMO awareness layer
- The research design — the Healthcare Worker Experience Survey (anonymous, 6 sections)
- All key product decisions — profession-aware logic, cultural sensitivity considerations, the name Ùlẹ̀rà AI and its Yoruba roots, the tagline *Ìtọ́jú fún àwọn olùtọ́jú àti ẹni tí wọn tọ́jú*

AI accelerated execution. The vision, the direction, and the lived understanding of the problem are entirely human — and entirely Nigerian.
