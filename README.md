## What is Ùlẹ̀rà AI?

Ùlẹ̀rà AI is a mobile-first intelligent health platform that addresses Nigeria's dual healthcare crisis — the **87% health insurance gap** and the **69% healthcare worker burnout rate**. Built by a licensed Community Health Extension Worker (CHEW) with 5 years of frontline experience in Oyo State, the app adapts to who you are: your profession, your location, and the season you are in.

The name **Ùlẹ̀rà** is drawn from the Yoruba word for health — *ilera* — written with full Yoruba diacritical marks, rooted in the Oyo dialect, the classical heart of the Yoruba language.

**The Insurance Gap:**
- Only **13% of Nigerians** have any form of health insurance
- **Over 190 million Nigerians** — 87% of the population — are completely uninsured
- **79% of adult Nigerians** pay entirely out of pocket every time they fall sick
- Free schemes like BHCPF and OYSHIA exist — but most Nigerians have never heard of them

**The Burnout Crisis:**
- **69% of Nigerian healthcare workers** are experiencing burnout
- Nigeria has only **2.9 doctors per 10,000 people** — far below the WHO recommendation of 17
- Healthcare workers carry impossible workloads with no digital support system
